WHO WE ARE

Uniqueness | Opportunity | Commitment

We believe that every individual is unique. What makes one unique is their Talent, Skill and Values.Cuaderno is a home for numerous talents, wide range of skill sets and the ones who treasure strong values that lasts for a lifetime. At Cuaderno you will seize the right opportunity to use your talents and skills, keeping a hold on moral and ethical values that build and unites us. With these opportunities you can explore more areas that will enhance your perspectives. When you excel in your domains you will always have the choice of sharing it. But, what completes it? Every individual devoid of any classification is bound to one thing in many ways - commitment. Personally, socially and organisationally we all have promises to keep, those promises if not kept might create distress among us. But if kept with love it can create more smiles, stronger trusts and sustainable changes. What’s lost with ignorance and laziness can be restored keeping our individuality and encountering correct opportunities.

CAMPAIGNS


• DONATE BOOKS

We conducted a book collection drive at selected colleges in Trivandrum for resetting the libraries at schools in Aranmula affected by flood.


• HOME FOR HOMELESS

To better the lives of Sudha Kumari, a blind woman , and her three young girls who lived in deplorable conditions in Balaramapuram, Cauderno along with Habitat Technology group constructed a new home for them.


• BLOOD DONATION CAMP

Cauderno in association with Regional Cancer Centre, Trivandrum organised a Blood donation camp on the 22 nd of March at Sasthamangalam.


• FREE EYE TESTING CAMP

We conducted a free eye testing camp at SRS U P School, Naruvamoodu.


• BED DONATION DRIVE

Organised a BED DONATION DRIVE at rehabilitation centre,Kattakada.
